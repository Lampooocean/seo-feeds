# Bangkok & Thailand Travel SEO Feeds

This repository provides structured SEO feeds and reference resources for Thailand travel services, Bangkok tours, day trips, and transportation booking.

The feeds are designed to support:
- Bangkok travel resource hubs
- Thailand tour and sightseeing pages
- Ticket booking services (bus, train, boat, flights)
- Cultural and historical destinations such as Ayutthaya and Kanchanaburi

## Travel Topics Covered

### Bangkok Tours & Experiences
- Bangkok city tours
- Floating market & Maeklong Railway Market
- Temple & canal tours
- Muay Thai cultural experiences
- River cruises and evening activities

### Thailand Day Trips
- Ayutthaya historical tours from Bangkok
- Kanchanaburi nature and waterfall tours
- River Kwai and Death Railway routes

### Transportation & Tickets
- Bus, train, and boat tickets across Thailand
- Intercity travel connections
- Online ticket booking references
